# Review Assignment and Decision Guide — v3.8.0

## Roles

### Author

Responds to review notes and prepares revisions.

### Editor

Coordinates review scope, assignments, decisions, and publication readiness.

### Reviewer

Evaluates the assigned review domain.

### Approver

Provides a decision counted toward the required approval threshold.

### Observer

Can follow the workflow without providing an approval decision.

## Decisions

### Approve

The reviewer considers the assigned domain ready.

### Approve with minor changes

The reviewer considers the work substantially ready but expects minor corrections.

### Request changes

The reviewer identifies changes required before approval.

### Reject

The reviewer considers the work unsuitable for the current release.

### Recuse

The reviewer cannot participate, usually because of a conflict or lack of appropriate expertise.

## Conflict disclosure

Reviewers should disclose financial, institutional, personal, authorship, supervisory, competitive, or other relevant conflicts.

The plugin records the disclosure but does not assess its adequacy.

## Decision quality

Decision notes should:

- identify the reviewed domain;
- distinguish required and optional changes;
- cite the relevant document section;
- avoid unsupported claims;
- disclose uncertainty;
- explain recusal or rejection;
- avoid exposing confidential information in public summaries.
