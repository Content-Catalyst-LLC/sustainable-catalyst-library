# Topic Coverage and Knowledge-Gap Analysis — v3.2.0

## Library report

Each Topic is measured across documents, Research Sources, Research Claims, and Research Projects.

Gap flags: `no-documents`, `no-sources`, `no-claims`, `no-projects`.

A simple score assigns 25 points for representation in each category. It measures platform coverage, not quality, accuracy, importance, or research completeness.

## Concept and project reports

Concept gaps: `no-claims`, `no-evidence-base`.

Project gaps: `no-topics`, `no-concepts` across the Project, attached Sources, connected documents, and Claims.

The library report also counts Sources that still have only legacy Source Topics.

## Caching

Public library coverage is cached for ten minutes. Semantic saves, Topic edits/assignments, relationship changes, status changes, deletions, and migration batches invalidate the cache. Private administrative reports are generated directly.

Coverage reports identify underdeveloped subjects and classification gaps. They do not determine truth, sufficiency of evidence, or publication priority.
