# Publication Readiness Checklist — v3.8.0

## Content

- Confirm every document is the intended version.
- Confirm document titles, authorship, dates, and metadata.
- Confirm abstracts and summaries.
- Confirm tables, figures, captions, and alt text.
- Confirm citations and bibliography.
- Confirm quotations and permissions.
- Confirm limitations and uncertainty.
- Confirm all required review notes are resolved.

## Review

- Confirm required reviewers participated.
- Confirm conflicts were disclosed and managed.
- Confirm approvals meet the threshold.
- Confirm no reject or change-request decision remains.
- Confirm document snapshots match.
- Confirm governance and quality gates.

## Rights and privacy

- Record license or rights statement.
- Confirm third-party rights.
- Review personal, confidential, and restricted information.
- Confirm donor and archival restrictions.
- Confirm legal holds and embargoes.
- Confirm accessibility.

## Release

- Assign version.
- Write release notes.
- Record DOI or canonical URL.
- Set embargo and publication time.
- Generate and inspect the manifest.
- Approve the package.
- Publish.
- Verify the public record.
- Preserve release history and supersession information.
