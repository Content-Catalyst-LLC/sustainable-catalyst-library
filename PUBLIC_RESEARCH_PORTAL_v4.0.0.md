# Public Research Portal — v4.0.0

## Public content

The portal can expose:

- public institutions;
- public research units;
- public documents;
- public pathways;
- public collections;
- public reviewed publications.

## Restricted content

The portal must not expose:

- raw private document content;
- private projects;
- evidence notes;
- review deliberations;
- quality issues;
- internal handoffs;
- API tokens;
- federation secrets;
- steward and author IDs;
- private filesystem paths.

## Shortcodes

```text
[sc_institutional_research_portal documents="8" units="12"]
[sc_institutional_search query="climate" types="document,pathway,collection"]
[sc_institutional_platform_status]
[sc_institutional_record id="123" type="document"]
```
