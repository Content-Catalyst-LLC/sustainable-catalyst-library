# Production Checklist — Knowledge Library v4.0.1

## Upgrade

- Confirm the repository is clean and currently reports v4.0.0 or v4.0.1.
- Run the installer and preserve the generated backup.
- Upload the generated v4.0.1 WordPress ZIP.
- Purge WordPress, server, Cloudflare, and browser caches.

## Discovery controls

- Open every domain card in Browse the Knowledge Architecture.
- Confirm the whole summary card and plus/minus indicator are clickable.
- Confirm one click changes the state exactly once.
- Confirm mouse, touch, Enter, and Space interactions.
- Confirm `aria-expanded` and plus/minus indicators follow the open state.
- Confirm nested topic grids become visible.
- Confirm controls continue working after search or discovery refresh.

## Entity rendering

- Confirm “Literature & Cultural Memory.”
- Confirm “Technology & Systems Intelligence.”
- Test single, double, decimal, and hexadecimal ampersand encoding.
- Confirm URLs and identifiers remain unchanged.

## Standalone repair removal

- Keep the standalone repair active for the initial v4.0.1 upload.
- Confirm the informational admin notice appears.
- Deactivate the standalone repair plugin.
- Purge caches and retest the Library.
- Delete the standalone repair only after the embedded repair is confirmed.

## Regression

- Run all 38 curated contracts.
- Confirm the Institutional Platform, API, review, intelligence, archive, quality, handoff, pathway, semantic, source, project, citation, OCR, and document layers remain available.
